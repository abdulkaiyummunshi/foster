For Campaign Creators

1. Planning Your Campaign

DEFINE YOUR PURPOSE

Be specific about what you need and why.

Instead of: "Help with medical bills"

Write: "Raise €15,000 for Sarah's cancer surgery scheduled December 2025"

SET A REALISTIC GOAL
Calculate your total need:
Medical treatment €10,000
Travel costs €1,500
Recovery supplies €500
Platform fees (~7%) €840
Total goal €12,840

Pro tip: It's better to exceed a modest goal than fall short of an ambitious one. You can always increase your goal later.

Rules:

- Keep under 80 characters

- Be specific and descriptive

- Include emotional hook

- Mention beneficiary by first name

2. Write a Compelling Campaign

CREATE A POWERFUL TITLE

Formula: [Action] + [Who] + [Specific Need] EXAMPLES:

EXAMPLES:

Weak: "Help Me Please"

Strong: "Help Sarah Fight Stage 3 Cancer"

Weak: "Community Project"

Instead of: "Help with medical bills"

TELL YOUR STORY EFFECTIVELY
Proven 5-part structure:

01

THE HOOK (2 SENTENCES)

Start with the most compelling, emotional part.

"Three weeks ago, my daughter Sarah was diagnosed with stage 3 breast cancer. At 32 years old with two children under 5, she's facing the fight of her life—and medical bills she can't afford."

02

THE BACKGROUND (2-3 PARAGRAPHS)

Who is the beneficiary? How did this happen?

03

THE SPECIFIC NEED (1-2 PARAGRAPHS)

Break down exactly what you're raising funds for:

  • "We're raising €15,200 to cover:
  • - Surgery: €8,000
  • - Chemotherapy: €4,500
  • - Radiation: €2,000
  • - Medications: €700"
04

THE IMPACT (1 PARAGRAPH)

Paint a picture of what success looks like.

05

THE ASK (1 PARAGRAPH)

Make a clear, specific request. Ask for donations AND shares

WRITING TIPS

Do:

  • - Use short paragraphs (2-4 sentences)
  • - Include specific details and numbers
  • - Write in first person
  • - Be honest and authentic
  • - Show emotion without manipulation

Don't:

  • - Use all caps or excessive punctuation
  • - Make vague claims
  • - Write long blocks of text
  • - Exaggerate or embellish

DONOR PROTECTION GUARANTEE

If You Donate to a Fraudulent Campaign:

Step 1: Report it using the "Report Campaign" button or email safety@Fosterer.com

Step 2: We investigate promptly upon receiving your report

Step 3: If fraud is confirmed:

• Campaign is immediately suspended

• Donors are notified

• Full refunds are issued

• Organizer's account is permanently banned

• Cases are reported to appropriate authorities

We're committed to making donors whole when fraud occurs.

How to Post Updates:
  • - Log into your dashboard
  • - Click "Post Update"
  • - Write your message (text, photos, videos)
  • - Choose visibility: all donors, public, or specific supporters
  • - Click publish (donors receive email notification)
What to Include in Updates:
  • - MILESTONE ACHIEVEMENTS: "We've reached 50% of our goal!"
  • - FUND USAGE: "Here's how we spent the first €1,000..."
  • - PROGRESS PHOTOS/VIDEOS: Show the impact being created
  • - THANK YOU MESSAGES: Acknowledge donors by name (with permission)
  • - CHALLENGES FACED: Honest communication about setbacks
  • - NEXT STEPS: What comes next and how close you are to the goal
Update Frequency:
  • - Minimum: Once every 7 days for active campaigns
  • - Recommended: Every 3-5 days during active fundraising
  • - Post-campaign: Final update showing complete outcome
