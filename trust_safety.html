At Fosterer trust isn't a marketing promise—it's our foundation. Every campaign and transaction is protected by verification, monitoring, and security systems designed to keep our global community safe.

Our Commitment to Safety

We've built one of the most secure crowdfunding platforms in the industry. Since launching in 2024, we've maintained industry-leading fraud prevention while ensuring that legitimate campaigns get approved quickly and efficiently.

Our approach balances rigorous security with accessibility—protecting donors without creating unnecessary barriers for organizers with genuine needs.

Campaign Verification

Every campaign on Fosterer undergoes verification before going live. We've designed our systems to work globally, ensuring security without exclusion.

IDENTITY VERIFICATION

What We Verify:

  • - Government-issued identification
  • - Proof of address
  • - Phone and email verification
  • - Identity matching through secure authentication

How It Works:

  • - Automated document verification using advanced technology
  • - Identity matching and authentication
  • - Cross-reference against relevant databases
  • - Manual review by trained specialists when needed

Users Without Standard Documentations

  • We recognize that not everyone has traditional government IDs. We offer alternative verification methods including:
  • - Mobile banking verification
  • - Community organization references
  • - Video verification with our team
  • - Regional verification partners

BENEFICIARY VERIFICATION (OPTIONAL)

For personal fundraisers, especially medical campaigns, we offer optional beneficiary verification to provide donors with additional confidence.

What Can Be Verified:

- Medical documentation and treatment needs

- Emergency situations and disaster impact

- Educational enrollment and expenses

- Identity of intended beneficiaries

Privacy Protection: All sensitive personal and medical information is encrypted, strictly access-controlled, and never displayed publicly without explicit consent.

ADVANCED FRAUD DETECTION

Our fraud detection systems analyze multiple risk factors to identify potential issues before campaigns go live.

Our Systems Evaluate:

- Content authenticity and consistency

- Account creation patterns and behavior

- Photo and video verification

- Network and relationship analysis

- Historical patterns and red flags

Campaigns flagged as higher-risk receive additional manual review by our Trust & Safety specialists.

Continuous Monitoring

Verification doesn't stop when a campaign goes live. We monitor all active campaigns continuously.

Our Monitoring Includes:

- Real-time transaction pattern analysis

- Content change detection

- User feedback and reports

- Withdrawal pattern reviews

- Ongoing compliance checks

Campaigns showing concerning patterns are reviewed promptly by our team.

How We Protect Donors

We never store payment card data on our servers. All payment processing is handled by industry-leading, certified payment processors including Stripe and PayPal.

Security Standards:

- TLS encryption for all data transmission

- AES-256 encryption for stored data

- End-to-end encryption for sensitive documents

- Secure authentication for all transactions

DONOR PROTECTION GUARANTEE

If You Donate to a Fraudulent Campaign:

Step 1: Report it using the "Report Campaign" button or email safety@Fosterer.com

Step 2: We investigate promptly upon receiving your report

Step 3: If fraud is confirmed:

• Campaign is immediately suspended

• Donors are notified

• Full refunds are issued

• Organizer's account is permanently banned

• Cases are reported to appropriate authorities

We're committed to making donors whole when fraud occurs.

REFUND & DISPUTE RESOLUTION

When You Can Request a Refund:

- Campaign is fraudulent or misrepresented

- Accidental duplicate donation

- Unauthorized transaction on your account

- Organizer misused funds contrary to campaign description

How to Request:

1. Contact the campaign organizer first (many issues resolve directly)

2. If unresolved, email safety@Fosterer.com with your transaction details and concern

3. We investigate and respond promptly

Your Rights:

You can always dispute charges with your bank or card issuer if needed.

STEP 2: VERIFY YOUR IDENTITY

Why Verification Matters:

Verification protects donors by confirming you're a real person with a legitimate need. Verified campaigns receive significantly more donations.

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS: 

Option A: Standard Verification (Most Users)

- Government-issued photo ID (passport, national ID card, driver's license)

- Proof of address (utility bill, bank statement, or official mail dated within 3 months)

- Phone number verification (SMS code)

- Email verification

- Selfie verification (holding your ID)

Option B: Alternative Verification (For Users Without Standard IDs)

- Mobile phone banking records or SIM registration

- Community organization vouching letter

- Video verification call with our team

- Alternative government documents (birth certificate + other supporting docs)

- Biometric verification through partner

01

Upload Documents Securely

  • Use our encrypted upload portal
  • Ensure documents are clear and fully visible
  • All corners and text must be readable
  • Color scans or high-quality photos preferred
02

Choose Campaign Type

  • Complete Selfie Verification
  • Take a selfie holding your ID next to your face
  • Ensure good lighting and clear image
  • Face and ID must both be clearly visible
  • Our AI matches your selfie to your ID photo
03

Phone & Email Verification

  • Enter your mobile number (receive SMS code)
  • Click verification link in email
  • Both must be from the same person/household
  • Processing Time:
  • Most legitimate campaigns are verified quickly, often within hours. You'll receive email notification when approved or if additional information is needed.

OPTIONAL: BENEFICIARY VERIFICATION (HIGHLY RECOMMENDED)

For medical, emergency, or personal hardship campaigns, voluntary beneficiary verification significantly increases donor confidence.

What We Verify:

- MEDICAL CAMPAIGNS: Hospital documentation, treatment bills, patient identity

- EMERGENCY RELIEF: Official documentation, news corroboration, location verification

- EDUCATION: Enrollment letters, tuition invoices, institution verification

- MEMORIAL/FUNERAL: Death certificate, funeral home documentation

Privacy Promise:

- Documents are encrypted and access-restricted

- You control what details are shown on your campaign page

- Beneficiary names can be anonymized if needed

How It Helps:

Campaigns with beneficiary verification see significantly higher donation rates and larger average contributions.

FUND MANAGEMENT OPTIONS

Standard Withdrawal (Default):

- Access funds anytime after the first donation

- No minimum withdrawal amount

- Withdraw partially or wait until goal is reached

- Transfer to your bank account or verified payment method

Milestone-Based Escrow (Optional):

Perfect for long-term projects like construction, ongoing medical treatment, or multi-phase community initiatives.

How It Works:

- Set specific milestones when creating your campaign

- Donations are held in secure escrow

- Upload proof when you complete each milestone (receipts, photos, reports)

- Our team reviews documentation

- Funds released upon approval

- Donors see exactly what their money funded

Why Use Milestones:

Significantly increases donor confidence, reduces fraud risk, creates accountability, and is better suited for high-value campaigns.
