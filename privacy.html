1. Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide
When you register, create or donate to a fundraiser, we may collect:

  • Contact Details: Name, email, phone number, address
  • Account Information: Username, password (stored in encrypted)
  • Payment Information: Credit/debit card details (processed securely via third-party providers like Stripe/PayPal)
  • Campaign Details: Fundraiser descriptions, photos, videos, bank info for withdrawals
  • Communications: Email, messages with support or other users

Automatically Collected Data

  • Device & Usage Data: IP address, browser type, operating system
  • Cookies & Tracking: We use cookies to improve user experience (you can disable them in browser settings)
  • Analytics: Non-identifiable data on campaign performance and visitor interactions

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide Services: Process donations, manage fundraisers, and facilitate payouts
  • Verify Identity: Prevent fraud and comply with legal obligations
  • Improve Platform: Analyze trends, optimize user experience, and develop new features
  • Communicate: Send updates, receipts, and respond to inquiries
  • Marketing: With consent, share newsletters or promotions (opt-out anytime)

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your data but may share it with:

  • Payment Processors: Stripe or PayPal (for transaction completion)
  • Service Providers: Hosting, analytics, and customer support tools
  • Law Enforcement: If required by law (e.g., in a subpoena for fraud investigations)
  • Campaign Visibility: Fundraiser details (not private info) are public to attract donors

4. Data Security

We implement measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption: For data in transit and at rest
