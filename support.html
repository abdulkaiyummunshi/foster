Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

For Campaign Organizers

How to Start a Fundraiser

  • 1. Sign up for a free Fosterer account.
  • 2. Click "Start a Fundraiser" and choose your category.
  • 3. Fill in your campaign details (title, story, goal amount).
  • 4. Submit for review (typically approved within 24 hours).
