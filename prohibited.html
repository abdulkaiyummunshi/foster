Fosterer maintains a safe, legal platform for all users. This policy outlines prohibited activities and content.

Violations may result in:

  • Campaign removal or suspension
  • Account termination
  • Fund withholding or return
  • Law enforcement reporting
  • Legal

Prohibited Content Policies

Illegal Activities, Weapons & Controlled Substances

CRIMINAL ACTIVITIES

Prohibited:

  • Any illegal activity under applicable laws
  • Money laundering, terrorist financing, or human trafficking
  • Drug trafficking or counterfeit goods
  • Fraud, cybercrime, or tax evasion
WEAPONS

Prohibited:

  • Firearms, ammunition, or weapon parts
  • Explosives or incendiary devices
  • Weapons accessories or instructions for creating weapons

Exception: Historical collectibles clearly for display only (with documentation)

CONTROLLED SUBSTANCES

Prohibited:

  • Illegal drugs or unauthorized prescription medications
  • Drug paraphernalia
  • Cannabis products (due to international restrictions)
  • Performance-enhancing drugs without prescription
GAMBLING

Prohibited:

  • Online gambling, lottery sales, sports betting, or casino games
  • Sweepstakes requiring payment

Exception: Nonprofit raffles with proper permits
