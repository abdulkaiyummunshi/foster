For Campaign Creators

1. Planning Your Campaign

DEFINE YOUR PURPOSE

Be specific about what you need and why.

Instead of: "Help with medical bills"
Write: "Raise €15,000 for Sarah's cancer surgery scheduled
December 2025"

SET A REALISTIC GOAL

  • Calculate your total need:

  • Medical treatment

    €10,000

  • Travel costs

    €1,500

  • Recovery supplies

    €500

  • Platform fees (~7%):

    €840

  • Total goal

    €12,840

Pro tip: It's better to exceed a modest goal than fall short of an ambitious one. You can always increase your goal later.

2. Write a Compelling Campaign

CREATE A POWERFUL TITLE

Formula: [Action] + [Who] + [Specific Need]

EXAMPLES:

Weak: "Help Me Please"
Strong: "Help Sarah Fight Stage 3 Cancer"

Weak: "Community Project"
Strong: "Build Clean Water Well for 500 Kenyan Families"

Rules:

- Keep under 80 characters

- Be specific and descriptive

- Include emotional hook

- Mention beneficiary by first name

TELL YOUR STORY EFFECTIVELY
Proven 5-part structure:

01

THE HOOK (2 SENTENCES)

Start with the most compelling, emotional part.

"Three weeks ago, my daughter Sarah was diagnosed with stage 3 breast cancer. At 32 years old with two children under 5, she's facing the fight of her life—and medical bills she can't afford."

02

THE BACKGROUND (2-3 PARAGRAPHS)

Who is the beneficiary? How did this happen?

03

THE SPECIFIC NEED (1-2 PARAGRAPHS)

Break down exactly what you're raising funds for:

"We're raising €15,200 to cover:

- Surgery: €8,000

- Chemotherapy: €4,500

- Radiation: €2,000

- Medications: €700"

04

THE IMPACT (1 PARAGRAPH)

Paint a picture of what success looks like.

05

THE ASK (1 PARAGRAPH)

Make a clear, specific request. Ask for donations AND shares

WRITING TIPS

Do:

- Use short paragraphs (2-4 sentences)

- Include specific details and numbers

- Write in first person

- Be honest and authentic

- Show emotion without manipulation

Don't:

- Use all caps or excessive punctuation

- Make vague claims

- Write long blocks of text

- Exaggerate or embellish

CHOOSE POWERFUL PHOTOS AND VIDEOS

Hero image of beneficiary or project

Context photo showing the situation

Personal photo humanizing the story

Documentation (bills, permits) if applicable

Photo requirements:

- High resolution (1200px+ wide)

- Well-lit and in focus

- Real people/situations (never stock photos)
