Fosterer is designed to make fundraising easy, transparent, and effective for individuals, nonprofits, and social causes. Whether you need help with medical bills, education, disaster relief, or creative projects, Fosterer provides the tools to turn your cause into a successful campaign. Here’s a step-by-step breakdown of how Fosterer works for both campaign organizers and donors:

For Campaign Organizers: How to Start a Fundraiser

Sign Up & Choose Your Cause

Create an account on Fosterer (it’s free!).
Select the category that best fits your fundraiser (medical,
education, disaster relief, etc.).

Tell Your Story

Write a compelling description – Explain why you’re raising
funds and how donations will be used.
Add photos/videos – Visuals help build trust and
engagement.
Set a fundraising goal – Be realistic about how much you
need and why.

Launch & Share Your Campaign

Once approved, your fundraiser goes live!
Promote it widely using social media, email, and messaging
apps.
Use Fosterer’s built-in sharing tools to reach more
supporters.

Receive Donations

Donors contribute directly to your campaign.
You can track progress in real-time and thank donors
publicly.
Withdraw funds securely via bank transfer, PayPal, or other
payout methods.

Update Supporters & Show Impact

Post updates to keep donors informed.
Share how funds are being used (e.g., medical treatment
progress, school construction photos).
Transparency builds trust and encourages more donations!

For Donors: How to Support a Cause

Browse Fundraisers

Explore verified campaigns in different categories.
Filter by urgency, location, or cause type.

Donate Securely

Choose a fundraiser that resonates with you.
Enter your donation amount and payment method (credit/
debit, PayPal, etc.).
Fosterer ensures secure transactions with encryption.

Get Updates & See Impact

Receive thank-you messages from campaigners.
Follow campaign updates to see how your contribution made
a difference.
Option to share the fundraiser to help it reach more people.
